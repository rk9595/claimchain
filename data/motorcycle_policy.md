# GEICO Motorcycle Insurance Policy Reference (Sample)

## Eligible Vehicles
Standard motorcycles, sport bikes, cruisers, touring bikes, trikes, mopeds, scooters, dirt bikes (on-road use), and ATVs (limited states). High-performance bikes (1200cc+ sport) underwritten with additional review.

## Coverages
- **Liability (BI/PD)**: Same structure as auto. Minimums set by state.
- **Collision**: Optional — covers damage from impact.
- **Comprehensive**: Optional — covers theft, vandalism, weather.
- **Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist**.
- **Medical Payments** (MedPay).
- **Accessory Coverage**: Custom parts, chrome, saddlebags, sidecars, stereo systems, helmets, riding apparel. Base $1,000 included; purchasable up to $30,000.
- **Total Loss Coverage**: In the first year after purchase, we pay the cost of a brand-new replacement motorcycle of the same make/model.
- **Transport Trailer Coverage**: Up to $1,500 for damage to a trailer used to transport the bike.

## Discounts
- Motorcycle Safety Foundation (MSF) course completion: up to 10%
- Multi-bike: up to 10%
- Homeowner: up to 5%
- Bundle with auto policy: up to 15%
- Mature rider (45+): variable
- Anti-theft lock / GPS tracker: up to 10%

## Seasonal Suspension
Riders in cold-weather states may suspend collision/comprehensive during winter and keep comprehensive-only for garaged storage. Liability may also be suspended if the bike is registered as PNO (Planned Non-Operation).

## Claims
- Lay-down claims (no other vehicle involved): photographs of both bike and road conditions required.
- Theft claims require a police report within 24 hours and the original key set.
- Approved repair shops include HOG-preferred and dealer service centers.
