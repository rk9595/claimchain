# GEICO Auto Insurance Policy Reference (Sample)

## Coverage Types

### Liability Coverage
- **Bodily Injury Liability**: Covers medical costs and legal fees if you injure another person in an accident. Minimum state limits vary; GEICO offers up to $500,000 per person / $1,000,000 per accident.
- **Property Damage Liability**: Covers damage you cause to other vehicles, fences, buildings, or property. Typical limits: $25,000 / $50,000 / $100,000 / $250,000.

### Physical Damage Coverage
- **Collision**: Covers damage to your car from impact with another vehicle or object. Standard deductibles: $250, $500, $1,000, $2,000.
- **Comprehensive**: Covers theft, vandalism, hail, fire, falling objects, animal strikes, and glass breakage. Same deductible tiers as Collision.

### Optional Coverages
- **Uninsured / Underinsured Motorist (UM/UIM)**: Pays when the at-fault driver has no or insufficient insurance.
- **Medical Payments (MedPay)** or **Personal Injury Protection (PIP)**: Covers medical bills for you and passengers regardless of fault.
- **Rental Reimbursement**: Up to $50/day, $1,500 maximum while your car is in a covered repair.
- **Emergency Roadside Service**: Towing up to 15 miles, lockout service, fuel delivery, jump starts.
- **Mechanical Breakdown Insurance (MBI)**: Available for vehicles under 15 months old / 15,000 miles. Covers major mechanical parts. Deductible $250.

## Rating Factors
Premiums are calculated using:
- Driving record (accidents, tickets in last 39 months)
- Credit-based insurance score (not available in CA, HI, MA)
- Vehicle make, model, year, and safety ratings
- Annual mileage and commute distance
- Garaging ZIP code
- Age, marital status, years licensed
- Prior insurance continuity

## Claims
- File online at geico.com/claims, via the GEICO app, or at 1-800-841-3000 (24/7).
- Photos of damage required for all collision/comprehensive claims.
- Rental car is dispatched within 24 hours of a covered claim if rental reimbursement coverage is active.
- Repair shop choice: you may choose any shop; GEICO's Auto Repair Xpress network guarantees parts and labor for as long as you own the vehicle.

## Cancellation
- Cancel any time by calling or in writing. Unused premium is refunded pro-rata.
- 10-day written notice required for non-payment cancellation.
- Full refund available within 10 days of policy start (free look period) if no claims filed.
