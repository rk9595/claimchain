# GEICO Homeowners Insurance Policy Reference (Sample)

## Core Coverages (HO-3 form)

| Coverage | What it Protects | Typical Limit |
|---|---|---|
| A - Dwelling | The home structure itself | Replacement cost of home |
| B - Other Structures | Detached garage, shed, fence | 10% of Coverage A |
| C - Personal Property | Furniture, electronics, clothing | 50-75% of Coverage A |
| D - Loss of Use | Additional living expenses while home is uninhabitable | 20% of Coverage A |
| E - Personal Liability | Lawsuits for bodily injury or property damage | $100,000 - $500,000 |
| F - Medical Payments | Guest medical bills regardless of fault | $1,000 - $5,000 |

## Covered Perils (HO-3 — Open Perils on dwelling)
Fire, lightning, windstorm, hail, explosion, riot, aircraft, vehicles, smoke, vandalism, theft, volcanic eruption, falling objects, weight of ice/snow, accidental discharge of water, freezing of plumbing, sudden tearing/cracking of HVAC, artificial electrical current.

## Typical Exclusions
- Flood damage (requires separate NFIP or private flood policy)
- Earthquake (available as endorsement or separate policy)
- Normal wear and tear, mold, pest infestation
- Intentional acts
- War, nuclear hazard
- Business activity on the premises (requires commercial policy)

## Endorsements and Add-ons
- **Scheduled Personal Property**: Higher limits on jewelry, art, firearms, collectibles. Itemized appraisal required for items over $2,500.
- **Water Backup & Sump Overflow**: $5,000-$25,000 limits for sewer backup.
- **Identity Recovery**: Up to $25,000 for restoration services after ID theft.
- **Service Line Coverage**: Underground wiring, plumbing, cable runs between the street and the home.
- **Equipment Breakdown**: Major appliances, HVAC, pool equipment.

## Discounts
- New home discount (home less than 10 years old): up to 25%
- Protective devices (monitored alarm, smoke detectors, deadbolts): up to 15%
- Claim-free discount: up to 20% after 3 consecutive claim-free years
- Bundle with auto: up to 25%
- Loyalty (5+ years): up to 10%

## Claims
- Deductible typically $1,000 - $2,500 (percentage deductibles may apply for wind/hail/hurricane in coastal states).
- 24/7 claim intake: 1-800-841-3000 or geico.com/claims.
- Temporary repairs to prevent further damage are reimbursed.
- Replacement Cost Value (RCV) is standard; Actual Cash Value (ACV) is available for lower premium.
